My academic and professional experiences led me to discover my passion for ensuring technology truly serves people. I graduated from Harvard, where I studied political science with a focus on technology and society. My coursework trained me to think critically about the different ways emerging technologies challenge our existing values and principles—and what happens when those systems don't align with our needs.

My professional experiences, however, revealed the real-world complexities that often prevent technology from working for people the way it's intended to. I'm working to understand and close the gap between intention and reality.

I keep up with technology, neuroscience, and demographic trends. Active weightlifter, 5K runner, and espresso connoisseur. I always have time to talk about my faith, Real Madrid, and F1.
